## Process

1. **Drafting**: Complete draft 10 days before publish date
2. **Review**: Review and revisions 7 days before publish date
3. **Finalization**: Final edits and media inclusion 3 days before publish date
4. **Scheduling**: Schedule for automatic publication

## Promotion Strategy

- Post to social media channels on day of publication
- Send newsletter update with new content weekly
- Engage with comments and questions within 24 hours

## Guidelines

- Each post should be 1,400-2,100 words (6-9 minute read)
- Include relevant code samples where appropriate
- Use at least 3 high-quality, relevant images
- Maintain diverse topic selection (no more than 2 consecutive posts in same category)
- Include practical takeaways readers can implement
- Link to relevant previous content to build internal references
