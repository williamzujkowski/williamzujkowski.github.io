About Me

Hi there! I’m William Zujkowski. I’ve spent over 15 years in IT, diving deep into cybersecurity, vulnerability management, DevSecOps, and cloud security. I’m happiest when I’m blending ethical hacking skills with HPC computing solutions or rolling up my sleeves to implement advanced threat detection.

Over the years, I’ve honed my craft in corporate, government, and research environments—everywhere from HPC clusters at NIH to the security platform at cloud.gov. I thrive on building cross-functional teams and safeguarding systems from emergent threats under tight compliance constraints.

I’m also deeply passionate about AI/ML for security automation and love tinkering with homelab setups so I can keep learning. Whenever I can, I blog about DevSecOps best practices, HPC infrastructure, and how machine learning is shaking up the threat intelligence world. If you share my curiosity, I’d love to chat. Let’s explore and create something secure, resilient, and cutting-edge together!
