About Me

Hello! I'm William Zujkowski, a dedicated cybersecurity engineer with over 15 years of IT experience in vulnerability management, DevSecOps, and cloud security. I thrive at the intersection of ethical hacking, advanced threat detection, and High-Performance Computing (HPC) solutions.

My background spans corporate, government, and research environments — from securing HPC clusters used for groundbreaking molecular dynamics at the National Institutes of Health to supporting the cloud.gov security platform at GSA. I specialize in building and guiding cross-functional security teams, maintaining compliance under tight deadlines, and ensuring systems remain resilient to emerging threats.

Beyond my day-to-day work, I’m passionate about AI/ML for security automation, exploring homelab setups to stay on the cutting edge, and sharing knowledge through blog posts and mentorship. If you’re curious about DevSecOps best practices, HPC infrastructure, or how machine learning can transform threat intelligence, feel free to reach out.
