# Hello, World!

Welcome to my brand new personal website! After weeks of planning, designing, and coding, I'm excited to finally share this space with you. This site will serve as my digital home where I'll share my thoughts, projects, and discoveries.

## What You'll Find Here

My website is divided into a few key sections:

### Blog

This blog section will feature regular posts about topics I'm passionate about, including technology, design, and personal development. I plan to publish new content at least twice a month, so be sure to check back often.

### Portfolio

In the [Portfolio](/projects/) section, you'll find a collection of my projects. Each project page includes detailed information about the problem I was trying to solve, the approach I took, and the final result. I've included both professional and personal projects to give you a comprehensive view of my capabilities.

### About Me

The [About Me](/about/) page provides more information about my background, skills, and interests. It's a great place to start if you want to learn more about who I am and what I do.

### Curated Links

The [Links](/links/) page is where I'll share interesting articles, tools, and resources I discover as I browse the web. Consider it my personal collection of digital bookmarks that I think others might find valuable.

## Technical Details

This site is built with:

- [11ty](https://www.11ty.dev/) - A simpler static site generator
- [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) - For hosting
- [GitHub Actions](https://github.com/features/actions) - For automated deployment

I chose this stack because it's lightweight, fast, and easy to maintain. The entire site is open source, and you can view the code on my [GitHub repository](https://github.com/your-username/my-website).
